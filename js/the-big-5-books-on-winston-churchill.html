  • The Splendid and the Vile: A Saga of Churchill, Family, and Defiance During the Blitz

    by Erik Larson

    "Spectacular . . . Larson, as America's most compelling popular historian, is at his best in this fast-moving, immensely readable, and even warmhearted account of the battle to save Britain."--The Christian Science Monitor

    Find out more at Bookshop.org or Amazon.com

  • Hero of the Empire: The Boer War, a Daring Escape, and the Making of Winston Churchill

    by Candice Millard

    Chosen as a Washington Post and New York Times Book Review Notable Book of 2016"A thrilling account...This book is an awesome nail-biter and top-notch character study rolled into one...Could someone be persuaded to make a movie about this episode of his life? I'd watch."--New York Times Critic Jennifer Senior's Top Ten Books of 2016

    Find out more at Bookshop.org or Amazon.com

  • Churchill: Walking with Destiny

    by Andrew Roberts

    "The best single-volume biography of Churchill yet written. . . . Roberts tells this story with great authority and not a little panache. He writes elegantly, with enjoyable flashes of tartness, and is in complete command both of his sources and the vast historiography."--Richard Aldous, The New York Times

    Find out more at Bookshop.org or Amazon.com

  • The Last Lion: Winston Spencer Churchill: Visions of Glory, 1874-1932

    by William Manchester

    "Absolutely magnificent . . . a delight to read . . . one of those books you devour line by line and word by word and finally hate to see end."--Russell Baker

    Find out more at Bookshop.org or Amazon.com
