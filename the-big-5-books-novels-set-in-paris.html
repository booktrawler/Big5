P.S. from Paris

by Marc Levy
From Marc Levy, the most-read French author alive today, comes a modern-day love story between a famous actress hiding in Paris and a bestselling writer lying to himself. They knew their friendship was going to be complicated, but love--and the City of Lights--just might find a way.
Find out more at Bookshop.org or Amazon.com

The Paris Wife

by Paula McLain
A deeply evocative story of ambition and betrayal, The Paris Wife captures the love affair between two unforgettable people: Ernest Hemingway and his wife Hadley. NAMED ONE OF THE BEST BOOKS OF THE YEAR BY People - Chicago Tribune - NPR - The Philadelphia Inquirer - Kirkus Reviews - The Toronto Sun - BookPage
Find out more at Bookshop.org or Amazon.com

The Red Notebook

by Antoine Laurain
"This is in equal parts an offbeat romance, detective story and a clarion call for metropolitans to look after their neighbours. . . . Reading The Red Notebook is a little like finding a gem among the bric-a-brac in a local brocante." --The Telegraph
Find out more at Bookshop.org or Amazon.com

The Elegance of the Hedgehog

by Muriel Barbery
"[The Elegance of the Hedgehog tells] a beautiful story with a large cast of fascinating, complicated characters whose behavior is delightfully unpredictable...No idea is too big or small to find a home in the Parisian apartment building where most of the characters live."--The Wall Street Journal
Find out more at Bookshop.org or Amazon.com

Sarah's Key

by Tatiana De Rosnay
"Sarah's Key unlocks the star crossed, heart thumping story of an American journalist in Paris and the 60-year-old secret that could destroy her marriage. This book will stay on your mind long after it's back on the shelf." --Risa Miller, author of Welcome to Heavenly Heights
Find out more at Bookshop.org or Amazon.com
